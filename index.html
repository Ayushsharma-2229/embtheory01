About EMB

EMB Embedded Systems Pvt. Ltd.( AESPL) is a Global Electronic System designs and electronic manufacturing company with corporate Headquarters in India and delivery centres in USA offering product development from conception to realization covering system architecture, Hardware Design (Electronics Engineering, Board Design , Silicon & FPGA Design , mechanical Design) Software (Embedded software , Cloud Applications , Enterprise Mobility applications ), Manufacturing (PCB Assemblies, Wire harness, box Build ) and Test Engineering ( Test Jigs, Test Automation, verification and validation) meeting Quality management standards for Automotive ( IATF 16949 ), Medical ( ISO 13485 ), Aerospace & Defence (AS9100D Scope: Manufacturer of Printed Circuit Board Assemblies, Electromechanical Assemblies & Cable Assemblies and Box Build) and Industrial ( ISO 9001) Products.

Understanding the market and technology trends gives EMB Electronic Product Engineering team ( Electronics Engineering, PCB Design Silicon & FPGA Design, Software Development and Mechanical Design) advantage in deciding and helping our valued customers in realizing their dream of new products which are highly researched and are successful in their launch.
